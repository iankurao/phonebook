Create a phonebook
------------------

The user should e able to:
-Create a contact
-Save a contact
-Edit and update a contact
-Delete a contact by ID
-Clear all contacts
-Find contact by ID
-Find all contacts


Pseudocode
-----------
-Create a class contact
-Create an initialization method
-Create methods to save, edit, delete, find by id, find all contacts etc.
-Write tests for each method
